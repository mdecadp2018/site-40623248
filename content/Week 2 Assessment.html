Week 2 Assessment

  1. Describe in your own words, what is design intent?
    (用自己的話來描述什麼是設計意圖?)

    Design Intent is the practice of developing your project’s objectives and requirements even before working on your design
    ( 設計意圖是在開始設計之前開發項目目標和要求的實踐 )

  2. What is the difference between sketch entities that are colored blue, black, and red?
    ( 藍色，黑色和紅色的草圖實體之間有什麼區別?)

    Blue means it is underconstrained, black means it is fully constrained, and red means it is overconstrained
    ( 藍色表示它不受約束，黑色表示它完全受約束，紅色表示它過度約束)

  3. What is automatic inferencing, and how is it utilized?
    ( 什麼是自動推理，它是如何使用的 )

    When Onshape automatically assigns constraints to sketch entities while sketching. It can be activated by hovering over an existing entity, or temporarily turned off by selecting the [shift] key.
    (Onshape在繪製草圖時自動為草圖實體指定約束時。 可以通過將鼠標停在現有實體上來激活，也可以暫時關閉選擇[shift]鍵。)

  4. Which feature is this and what is it used for: ?
    (這個功能是什麼，用於什麼)

    This is the plane tool, used to create new planes in addition to the default Top/Right/Front planes
    (這是平面工具，用於創建新平面，除了默認的上視/右視/前視平面)

  5. How are fillets and chamfers similar? How are they different?
    (圓角和倒角有何相似之處？ 他們有什麼不同？)

    They are the same in that both features are applied to edges only, they are different in that a fillet creates a round profile, and a chamfer creates a flat profile
    (這兩個特徵都僅應用於邊緣，而圓角創建圓形輪廓，倒角創建平面輪廓)
